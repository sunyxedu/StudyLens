One-time setup

StudyLens reads your courses from Scientia, Panopto, the DOC exams site, and EdStem. The server can't open a browser for you, so capture your logins once on your own machine and upload them to this account:

  1. In a terminal on your computer, inside the StudyLens project folder, run: 
    uv run studylens-push-browser-state
  2. Enter your StudyLens username and password. Once they check out, a browser window opens — sign into Scientia, Panopto, the DOC exams site, and EdStem, pressing Enter in the terminal after each page loads.
  3. It uploads your logins here. Come back and click Continue to discover your courses.

You can skip the prompts by exporting STUDYLENS_USERNAME and STUDYLENS_PASSWORD first.
